Welcome to Paws & Tails

Looking for a furry companion to share your life with? You've come to the right place! Our adoption center is filled with loving pets looking for a forever home. Whether you're interested in a playful pup, a cuddly kitten, or a more mature animal with lots of love to give, we're here to help you find the perfect match.

How It Works

  1. Browse Our Available Pets: Check out our adorable pets ready for adoption below.
  2. Submit an Application: Found someone you like? Fill out our adoption application form, and we’ll get in touch with you.
  3. Meet & Greet: If you’re a good fit, we’ll schedule a meet-and-greet to see how you and your potential pet get along.
  4. Bring Your New Pet Home: After approval, you’ll be ready to bring your new family member home!
